Welcome to the Placement Cell!

The Placement Cell is dedicated to connecting students with top companies and career opportunities. We provide resources, training, and guidance to prepare students for successful careers, working closely with industry leaders to ensure they’re equipped for professional success.

About Placement cell

At Placement Cell, we are committed to empowering students with the skills and connections they need to thrive in their professional lives. Our team collaborates with industry leaders to offer comprehensive career support, from resume workshops and interview training to internships and job placements. We aim to create a seamless pathway from education to employment, ensuring every student reaches their full potential.

Recruitment Process

1

Explore Opportunities

Find job and internship openings posted on our website and stay updated through our alerts.
2

Apply

Submit your application, including your resume, for roles that match your skills and interests.
3

Shortlisting

If you meet the criteria, you’ll be shortlisted and notified about the next steps.
4

Assessments

Complete any required tests or assignments to demonstrate your abilities.
5

Interviews

Attend technical and HR interviews to showcase your knowledge and fit for the company.
6

Receive an Offer

If selected, you’ll receive a detailed job offer. Review and accept it to start your new role.
7

Onboarding

We’ll help you get started with orientation and resources to ensure a smooth transition.
